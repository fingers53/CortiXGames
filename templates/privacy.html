Privacy Policy

We only collect the information needed to run the games and leaderboards.

  • Account data: Username, optional password hash, and optional profile fields (country, gender, age range, handedness).
  • Gameplay data: Scores, timing metrics, and accuracy for each round of each game.
  • Storage: Data is stored in our database; we do not sell or share it with third parties.

Security

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt.
  • Score submissions use CSRF tokens and server-side validation.
  • We filter trivial math questions to avoid low-quality content.

Your Choices

  • You can play as a guest without creating an account.
  • You can request account removal by contacting support.
  • Public leaderboards show usernames only.
